Important Books

Choosing the right books is an important step in GATE ECE preparation. Standard textbooks help in building strong concepts and solving a variety of problems that are commonly asked in the exam.

  • Engineering Mathematics:
    • Engineering Mathematics – B.S. Grewal
    • Higher Engineering Mathematics – B.V. Ramana
  • Networks:
    • Network Analysis – M.E. Van Valkenburg
    • Network Analysis – A. Sudhakar
  • Signals and Systems:
    • Signals and Systems – Oppenheim & Willsky
    • Signals and Systems – B.P. Lathi
  • Electronic Devices:
    • Electronic Devices and Circuit Theory – Boylestad & Nashelsky
    • Microelectronic Circuits – Sedra & Smith
  • Analog Circuits:
    • Microelectronic Circuits – Sedra & Smith
    • Integrated Electronics – Millman & Halkias
  • Digital Circuits:
    • Digital Design – M. Morris Mano
    • Digital Electronics – R.P. Jain
  • Control Systems:
    • Control Systems Engineering – Nagrath & Gopal
    • Modern Control Engineering – K. Ogata
  • Communications:
    • Communication Systems – Simon Haykin
    • Principles of Communication Systems – Taub & Schilling
  • Electromagnetics:
    • Engineering Electromagnetics – William H. Hayt
    • Electromagnetics – Matthew Sadiku
